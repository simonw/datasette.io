It's about time the Datasette project had its own blog.

In addition to announcements about Datasette itself, we also plan to include posts from community members highlighting their own projects that fit into the wider Datasette ecosystem.

You can [subscribe to the Atom feed](https://datasette.io/content/feed.atom), follow Datasette on [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/datasette.io) or [Mastodon](https://fosstodon.org/@datasette) or [Twitter](https://x.com/datasetteproj), or [subscribe via email](https://datasette.substack.com) to stay updated on what's going on.
